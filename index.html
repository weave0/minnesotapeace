What I Offer

ADR Services

Qualified under Minnesota Rule 114, I provide flexible, fully-neutral alternative dispute resolution — tailored to your family's circumstances, not a one-size-fits-all formula.

Mediation

Neutral, confidential facilitation that keeps both parties at the table — creating agreements that reflect your family's real needs, not a courtroom's timetable.

Med-Arb

We start collaboratively. If an impasse is reached, I shift to binding arbitration — efficiency and finality, without the delays of re-starting in a new venue.

Arb-Med

An arbitration-derived framework provides structure, then mediation humanizes the outcome — effective when complex issues need both definition and flexibility.

Facilitative / Hybrid

No two families are the same. A tailored blend of facilitation styles matched to your communication dynamics, history, and specific goals for resolution.

What to Expect

A Simple, Supportive Process

Mediation doesn't have to feel intimidating. Here's how we move from first contact to resolution — at a pace that works for everyone involved.

01

Initial Consultation

A confidential, no-pressure conversation about your situation. We discuss your goals and whether mediation is the right path forward.

02

Mediation Session(s)

Structured, neutral sessions focused on the real issues — parenting arrangements, support, asset division. At your pace, on your terms.

03

Written Agreement

When agreement is reached, we document it clearly and completely. Your attorneys can then incorporate it into final legal filings.
